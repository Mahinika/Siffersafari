## Summary
Siffersafari is designed to work offline-first and store progress locally on the device.

## Data we store
- User profiles (name, selected grade, progress, stats)
- Quiz history (results, timestamps, points)
- Settings (sound/music toggles, onboarding completion, parent PIN)

This data is stored locally on the device using on-device storage.

## Data we collect
- No analytics collection is included in the MVP.
- No crash reporting is included in the MVP.
- No third-party advertising SDKs are included in the MVP.

## Network
- The MVP does not require an account.
- The app is intended to function without internet connectivity.
